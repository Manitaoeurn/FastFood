Burger

A burger is a dish consisting of a flat round cake of minced beef, or sometimes another savoury ingredient, that is fried or grilled and served in a split bun or roll with various condiments and toppings.

French Fries

French fries (or simply fries, also known as chips among other names) are batonnet or julienne-cut deep-fried potatoes of disputed origin. They are prepared by cutting potatoes into even strips, drying them, and frying them, usually in a deep fryer.

RECIPE:

Potato, Vinegar, Salt, Oil

Pasta

Pasta, any of several starchy food preparations (pasta alimentaria) frequently associated with Italian cuisine and made from semolina, the granular product obtained from the endosperm of a type of wheat called durum, and containing a large proportion of gluten (elastic protein).

RECIPE:

It so simple and fun and only requires 2 ingredients: You will need Tipo 00 flour (fine pasta flour) and eggs. One persons portion is 1 egg to 100g of flour. I made pasta for 5 of us so used 500g flour & 5 eggs.
